Enstructo Incorporated is an eLearning community where information security and data privacy professionals educate each other by publishing online courses that meet the Continuing Professional Education (CPE) requirements of certifying organizations like CompTIA, (ISC)2, ISACA, and IAPP.

The Enstructo eLearning Platform offers a dynamically scaled user experience, optimized for smartphones, tablets and desktop web browsers. Course content remains the intellectual property of the course creator, may be loaded into the Enstructo eLearning Platform as HTML, audio, video, and/or SCORM conformant packages, and is vetted for quality before it is published.

Courses are $50 for each credit hour, and course creators (“Enstructors”) receive monthly payments of 50% of the revenue generated by enrollments in the courses they publish.
