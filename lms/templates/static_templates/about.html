Enstructo Incorporated is an eLearning community where information security and data privacy professionals educate each other by publishing online courses that meet the Continuing Professional Education (CPE) requirements of certifying organizations like (ISC)2, ISACA, and IAPP.

Course content is vetted for quality, remains the intellectual property of the course creator, and may be loaded into the Enstructo platform as HTML, audio, video, and/or SCORM conformant packages.

Courses are $50 for each credit hour, and course creators (“Enstructors”) receive monthly payments of 50% of the revenue generated by enrollments in the courses they publish.
